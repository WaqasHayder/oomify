Can you work on an existing Shopify theme instead of building from scratch? +
Yes. I can refine an existing theme, rebuild key sections, improve the homepage and product flow, or fully redesign the store depending on what will create the strongest outcome.
Can you add premium motion without making the store feel heavy? +
Yes. The goal is refined motion, not noise. Animations are used to improve perception, hierarchy, and presentation while keeping performance in mind.
Do you also help with CRO and page structure, not just visuals? +
Yes. Layout decisions are made around offer clarity, trust signals, content flow, and buying momentum. Good design without conversion thinking is incomplete.
What kind of businesses are the best fit for this style of build? +
Premium DTC brands, product-led startups, beauty, fashion, wellness, accessories, lifestyle brands, and any store that wants to move upmarket visually while improving performance.
